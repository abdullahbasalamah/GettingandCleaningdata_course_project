# Activity Labels
* (value 1)= WALKING : subject walking during the measurement
* (value 2)= WALKING_UPSTAIRS : subject walking up a staircase during the measurement
* (value 3)= WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS : subject walking down a staircase during the measurement
* (value 4)= SITTING : subject walking sitting during the measurement
* (value 5)= STANDING : subject walking standing during the measurement
* (value 6)= LAYING : subject walking laying down during the measurement
